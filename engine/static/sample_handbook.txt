Acme Robotics — Employee Handbook (excerpt)

Vacation policy

Full-time employees accrue 1.75 vacation days per month, capped at 28 days.
Unused days roll over up to a maximum of 10 days into the next calendar year.
Vacation requests longer than 5 consecutive days require manager approval at
least 3 weeks in advance.

Remote work

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements
require VP approval and a home-office security review. Company laptops must
use full-disk encryption and the corporate VPN when off-site.

Expense reimbursement

Business expenses under $75 do not require receipts. All expenses must be
submitted within 60 days via the Concur portal. Per-diem for travel is $55
domestic and $80 international.

Security policy

Confidential documents must never be uploaded to third-party cloud services,
including AI assistants and translation tools. All document processing must
happen on approved on-premise infrastructure. Violations are subject to
disciplinary action up to termination.

On-call rotation

Engineers join the on-call rotation after 3 months of employment. On-call
weeks are compensated with 1 extra vacation day and a $250 stipend. Response
time SLA is 15 minutes for P1 incidents and 2 hours for P2.
